Quick Start Guide

Get started in minutes

1

Download

Windows — Save tchekly.exe to your PC. SmartScreen may appear — click "More info → Run anyway".

Android — Download Tchekly.apk and open it. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" if prompted.

2

Launch

Double-click the .exe. The app opens in a native desktop window. You get a 3-day free trial with all features unlocked.

3

Check Emails

Go to Check Emails, upload a .txt file (format: email:password per line), optionally add proxies and keywords, then click Start.

4

View Results

Valid emails save to results/good_emails/. Keyword matches go to results/keyword_matches/. Intel reports to results/intel/.

5

License

After the 3-day trial, purchase a license from the License page inside the app. $8 USDT/month via NOWPayments (BSC/BEP20).

6

Update

Download the latest .exe from this page and replace your old file. Your license, settings, and results are preserved.

FAQ

Common questions answered

Does Tchekly work on Linux, Mac, or Android?
Tchekly runs on Windows 10/11 (64-bit) and Android 8+. Linux and Mac are not supported.
Why does SmartScreen block the .exe?
The app is not code-signed. Click "More info" → "Run anyway" to proceed. The app is safe — it runs 100% locally with no data collection.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All processing is done locally on your machine. The app does not phone home, collect data, or send anything to external servers.
Can I use proxies?
Yes. The app supports SOCKS4, SOCKS5, and HTTP proxies. You can also use the built-in free proxy fetcher which gathers working proxies from 20+ sources automatically.
How do I buy a license?
Open the License page inside the app and click "Buy License". Pay $8 USDT/month via NOWPayments (BSC/BEP20 network). After payment, click "Activate Now" to verify your HWID-locked license.
How do I update the app?
Just download the latest tchekly.exe from this page and replace your existing file. Your license, settings, and results are stored separately and will be preserved.
