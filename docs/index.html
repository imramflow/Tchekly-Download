Quick Start Guide

Get started in minutes

1

Download

Click the download button and save tchekly.exe to your PC. Windows may show SmartScreen — click "More info → Run anyway".

2

Launch

Double-click the .exe file. The app opens in a native desktop window with a splash screen. You get a 3-day free trial.

3

Check Emails

Go to "Check Emails", upload a .txt file (format: email:password per line), optionally add proxies and keywords, then click Start.

4

View Results

Valid emails save to results/good_emails/. Keyword matches save to results/keyword_matches/. Intel reports save to results/intel/.

6

Update

Download the latest .exe from this page and replace your old one. Your license and settings are preserved.

FAQ

Common questions answered

Does Tchekly work on Linux or Mac?
No. Tchekly is a Windows-only desktop application built with pywebview (native WebView2 window). It requires Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit).
Why does SmartScreen block the .exe?
The app uses PyArmor obfuscation and is not code-signed. Click "More info" → "Run anyway" to proceed. The app is safe — it runs 100% locally with no data collection.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All processing is done locally on your machine. The app does not phone home, collect data, or send anything to external servers. The only network requests are credential validation (which you explicitly request) and fetching remote config (banners only). Passwords are never logged — only email addresses appear in logs.
Can I use proxies?
Yes. The app supports SOCKS4, SOCKS5, and HTTP proxies. You can also use the built-in free proxy fetcher which gathers working proxies from 20+ sources automatically.
How do I buy a license?
Open the License page inside the app and click "Buy License". Pay $8 USDT/month via NOWPayments (BSC/BEP20 network). After payment, click "I've Sent — Activate Now" to verify and activate.
How do I update the app?
Just download the latest tchekly.exe from this page and replace your existing file. Your license, settings, and results are stored separately and will be preserved.
